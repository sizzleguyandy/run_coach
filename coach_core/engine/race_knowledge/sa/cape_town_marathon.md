# Sanlam Cape Town Marathon

## The Course
The race is a fast, predominantly flat and AIMS-certified city marathon. It starts and finishes at the Green Point Stadium, looping through the city center and the leafy southern suburbs before returning along the coast. The iconic Table Mountain provides a stunning, ever-present backdrop throughout the route. Total elevation gain is approximately **282 metres**.

## Key Climbs / Challenges
- The course is designed for speed, with only a few gentle undulations in the southern suburbs. The main challenge is maintaining focus on a fast, flat course and dealing with potential wind along the coastal sections.

## Aid Stations
Aid stations are positioned at regular intervals along the route, providing water and energy drinks. The event is well-organized with a strong focus on supporting runners aiming for personal bests.

## Pacing Advice
This course is ideal for an evenly paced or negative split marathon. Aim to lock into your goal marathon pace early and maintain it with discipline. The flat, fast nature of the course allows for a consistent rhythm without the need to manage major hills.

## Gear Notes
Lightweight, fast road racing shoes are the ideal choice. No special gear is required. Be prepared for potential wind by wearing form-fitting clothing.

## Tips for TR3D Runners
This is an excellent opportunity to test your endurance at a steady state. Focus on maintaining a consistent Threshold (T) pace for as long as possible. The flat course is perfect for executing a perfectly paced marathon and practicing efficient running economy.
