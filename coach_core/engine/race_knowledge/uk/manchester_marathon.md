# adidas Manchester Marathon

## The Course
The race begins in the Trafford area near Old Trafford stadium. It features a three-mile loop through Manchester City Centre via Deansgate before heading southwest through the suburbs of Sale and Timperley, returning to finish near Old Trafford. With an elevation gain of only **54 metres**, it is one of the flattest marathons in the UK.

## Key Climbs / Challenges
- This is one of the flattest marathon courses in the UK, with minimal elevation change. The main challenge is maintaining focus on a flat, straight course.

## Aid Stations
Aid stations are positioned at regular intervals, providing water and sports drinks. While the exact offerings may vary, runners can expect a well-supported course.

## Pacing Advice
This course is ideal for even or negative splitting due to its flat profile. Lock into your goal marathon pace early and hold it. The flat terrain makes it easy to maintain a consistent rhythm.

## Gear Notes
Lightweight, fast road shoes are perfect. No special gear is required due to the course's flat and paved nature.

## Tips for TR3D Runners
This is an excellent course to test your endurance at a steady state. Aim to run the entire race at or slightly below your Threshold (T) pace. Use the flat terrain to practice efficient running economy.
